Privacy Policy for Mute

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Developer: J B Lopez

Mute is a privacy-first call screening application for Android. It helps users identify trusted callers and reduce unwanted or suspicious calls while keeping personal information on the user's device whenever possible.

Privacy First

Call screening decisions are processed entirely on your device using Android's official Call Screening APIs and locally stored information. Mute does not upload your contacts, call history, or call screening decisions to remote servers.

Information We Access

Contacts

If you grant the Contacts permission, Mute accesses your contacts only to determine whether an incoming caller is one of your saved contacts. Contact information is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded or shared.

Incoming Call Information

Android provides the incoming phone number required for call screening. Mute uses this information locally to determine whether the call should be allowed, logged, or blocked according to your settings.

Local Call History

Mute stores call screening history locally on your device so you can review previous screening decisions. This information remains on your device unless you delete it or uninstall the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

Mute does not:

  • Create user accounts
  • Require sign-in
  • Upload contacts
  • Upload call history
  • Sell personal information
  • Share personal information with third parties
  • Use advertising identifiers
  • Display advertisements
  • Track users across apps or websites

Internet Connectivity

Mute is designed to operate without requiring continuous internet connectivity for its core call screening functionality. Future optional online features may be introduced. If that happens, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features become available.

Permissions Used

  • READ_CONTACTS — Used only to identify trusted contacts during call screening.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Used to display notifications when supported by your Android version.
  • Android Call Screening Role — Required to perform call screening using Android's official Call Screening framework.

Data Security

Because call screening is processed locally on your device, your personal data remains under your control. Mute is designed to minimize data collection and avoid unnecessary transmission of personal information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date shown at the top of this page.
